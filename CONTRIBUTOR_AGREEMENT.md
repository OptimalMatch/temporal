**"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including any modifications or additions to existing work, that you submit to the Temporal project.

**"Submit"** means any form of electronic, verbal, or written communication sent to the project, including but not limited to communication on electronic mailing lists, source code control systems, and issue tracking systems.

You retain the right to use your Contribution for any purpose, provided that such use does not conflict with the rights assigned to Unidatum Integrated Products LLC under this Agreement.

You represent and warrant that:

a) You are the original author of the Contribution and have the legal right to assign the copyright as set forth in this Agreement.

b) Your Contribution does not, to the best of your knowledge, violate any third party's copyrights, trademarks, patents, or other intellectual property rights.

By making a Contribution to this project, you indicate your acceptance of this Agreement. You must include the following statement in your pull request or commit message:

```

and agree to the terms of the Contributor Assignment Agreement (CAA).

Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
```

If you are making a Contribution on behalf of your employer or another legal entity, an authorized representative of that entity must sign a Corporate Contributor Assignment Agreement. Please contact the project maintainers for the corporate CAA form.
